Vision behind our logo

The logo features a distinctive image of two hands holding a circle.The circle represents a globe, and the hands are meant to convey a sense of connectivity and communication across the world. they are designed to represent diversity and inclusion. The two hands are different colors, one green and one saffron are intended to represent different people and cultures coming together.This reflects HHC's commitment to building a diverse and inclusive workplace and fostering innovation through a broad range of perspectives and experiences.

Saffron: The saffron color at the top of the flag represents courage, sacrifice, and the spirit of renunciation. It also symbolizes the Hindu religion, which is the majority religion in India.
White: The white color in the middle of the flag represents truth, purity, and peace. It also represents the minority religions in India, such as Islam and Christianity.
Green: The green color at the bottom of the flag represents growth, fertility, and auspiciousness. It also represents the agricultural and rural heritage of India.
Hands: Together, the colors and the circle hands on the NGO LOGO represent the unity and diversity of the World, and its aspiration towards peace, progress, and prosperity.
